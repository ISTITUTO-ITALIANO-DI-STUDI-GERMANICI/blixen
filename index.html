Karen Blixen Aligned

English–Danish prototype for aligned texts and TEI/XML workflows

Karen Blixen (Isak Dinesen) was a Danish author renowned for luminous prose and mythic storytelling. Her work bridges cultures, memory, and fate, from East Africa to Copenhagen salons. This edition aligns Danish originals with English translations to support comparative reading, annotation, scholarly reuse, and teaching.
